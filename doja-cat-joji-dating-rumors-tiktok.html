SLEUTHS on social media are speculating about an alleged relationship between two well-known performers.

Fans of rapper and singer, Doja Cat, and former Internet personality - turned musician - Joji, took to TikTok to make guesses surrounding the pair's rumored romance.

Why do fans think Doja Cat and Joji are dating?

In February of 2022, speculation swirled among social media fans of Doja Cat and Joji - which alleged a secret romance between the two.

The rumors come on the heels of a few online interactions the two performers had in the past.

On January 13, 2022, Doja Cat tweeted at Joji and said: "joji wyd u doing."

On November 16, 2021, the rapper replied to a Joji fan account on Twitter which sent pop music sleuths into overdrive.

The fan page said: "i know doja cat stalks me so hi doja this post is for you @DojaCat."

She replied: "keep it the f*** down I'm not doing this w u right now."

Doja added: "ur loud ur really loud right now."

The reports continued to spread after another Joji fan account posted a video that included a series of speculative tweets to their TikTok page - which accumulated over three million views.

Neither of the two musicians confirmed they are in a relationship with one another.

Who is Joji?

Born on September 18, 1992 - Joji found Internet popularity through his YouTube accounts, TVFilthyFrank and DizastaMusic.

On YouTube, he posted numerous music parodies and sketch comedy routines as Filthy Frank.

Joji - real name George Kusonoki Miller - retired his online personas and channels to pursue a music career.

Under his former stage name, Pink Guy, he released his debut studio album - Pink Season - in January of 2017.

What is Doja Cat's net worth?

Doja Cat rose to fame in the late 2010s for her various music releases.

Her recognizable hit songs include Say So, Juicy, You Right, Need to Know, Kiss Me More, and Get Into It (Yuh).

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the performer amassed a wealth of $8million.

Doja is decorated with many nominations from the Grammy's, Billboard, MTV, iHeartRadio, and the BRIT's.
