When is John Mulaney performing at NYCs City Winery?

July 2024 · 3 minute read

JOHN Mulaney returned to the stage in NYC after a brief stint in rehab following his battle with addiction.

It was announced that Mulaney and his wife Anna Marie Tendler are set to divorce after six years of marriage.

Are John Mulaney and his wife divorcing?

According to Page Six, John asked for a divorce from Anna three months ago.

A spokesman for the former Saturday Night Live writer confirmed the split and added: “John will not have any further comment as he continues to focus on his recovery and getting back to work.”

Anna - who is an artist, photographer, and writer - also commented via her own spokesperson, as she said: “I am heartbroken that John has decided to end our marriage. I wish him support and success as he continues his recovery.

When is John Mulaney performing at NYC’s City Winery?

Mulaney is scheduled to perform at the City Winery for May 10 through May 14.

This comes after Mulaney's 60 day stint Pennsylvania rehab facility for his ongoing battle with drug and alcohol addiction.

Mulaney has struggled with alcohol and drugs since he was 12 years old, citing a lack of confidence in adolescence to his usage.

By February of 2021, Mulaney had completed his 60 day program, and transitioned into outpatient sober care.

Before his time in rehab, Mulaney had considerably slowed his workload while he was struggling.

The man behind multiple comedy specials, the hit Netflix shows Bigmouth and John Mulaney & the Sack Lunch Bunch had taken on less and less projects.

In November of 2020, Mulaney was writing for his old Saturday Night Live coworker Seth Meyers who hosts his own late night television show.

Mulaney admitted himself into the facility to seek treatment in December 2020.

Where can I buy tickets to see John Mulaney?

Unfortunately, Mulaney's first shows since returning to comedy almost immediately sold out.

As a large number of American citizens have received or are receiving the Covid-19 vaccination; live events are beginning to return in part.

Comedy shows had been hosted during the pandemic, often in outdoor spaces that followed CDC social distancing guidelines.

Per the official event listing for Mulaney's residency guests must be, "either be fully vaccinated or have received a negative Covid-19 test prior to the event.”
