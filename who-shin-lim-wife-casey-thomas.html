Who is Shin Lim's wife Casey Thomas?

Born in Sydney, Australia; Casey Thomas is an entertainer and dancer who has been performing since the age of four.

She attended the Newtown Performing Arts School, majoring in both Contemporary Dance and Musical Theatre.

After graduating high school, Casey went on to earn a Diploma of Performing Arts in Musical Theatre from ED5.

Starting in her teenage years, Casey modeled for GirlFriend Magazine and appeared in a variety of advertisements across Australian television.

Casey starred as a dancer for Minnie Cooper and her Tiny Teeny Tappers on Australia's Got Talent.

She was offered a residency in Macau to perform with illusionist Fran Harary at his House of Magic Show, where she eventually met and began dating Shin Lim.

The entertainer is now a creative director and camera switcher for the LIMITLESS show at the Mirage Hotel in Las Vegas, Nevada.

With over 29,0000 Instagram followers, Casey is known as "The Cardcaptor."

When did Shin Lim and Casey Thomas get married?

On August 19, 2019, Shin Lim and Casey Thomas tied the knot at a resort in Hawaii.

The two first began dating sometime in 2016.

"To the only person in the world that I want beside me every day and every night for the rest of my life," Casey wrote via Instagram on the couple's one-year anniversary.

"Having you always by my side makes me the happiest, most grateful and luckiest girl in the world!"

Who is Shin Lim?

Born on September 25, 1991, Shin Lim's full name is Liang-Shun Lim.

After teaching himself magic and sharing videos on YouTube, Shin won the Fédération Internationale des Sociétés Magiques World Championship for Close-up Card Magic in 2015.
