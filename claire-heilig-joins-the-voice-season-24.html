The Bachelor Star Claire Heilig Joins The Voice Season 24

June 2024 · 4 minute read

American television personality Claire Heilig has just announced her participation in the upcoming season of The Voice.

On September 16, 2023, Heilig took to Instagram to share an exciting update with her followers. She uploaded a vibrant poster from The Voice and wasted no time in spilling the beans. In her caption, she shared she had auditioned for NBC’s The Voice.

With enthusiasm, the TV personality encouraged her followers to mark their calendars and join her on this thrilling journey, urging them to tune in for the upcoming season, which was set to kick off on September 25, 2023, on NBC and would also be available for streaming on Peacock.

Upon seeing the post, Heilig’s fans and friends were overcome with excitement and couldn’t resist expressing their enthusiasm through a flurry of heartfelt comments.

Susie Evans exclaimed, “STOP!! I am unwell!!” while Elizabeth Corrigan joyfully chimed in with a simple “YAY!!” Their reactions conveyed their genuine happiness and support for Heilig’s exciting announcement.

The Bachelor Nation also joined in to express their excitement, with one saying, “OMG, I haven’t watched in years but will be now!” while another enthusiastic fan exclaimed, “I literally yelled out loud! So excited for you, will be cheering you on!”

Just a few days after the revelation, the television personality delighted her followers by posting another photo of herself.

In the snapshot, she was comfortably seated in the judge’s chair on the set of The Voice, and she exuded confidence as she pressed the buzzer, adding an extra layer of excitement to the scene.

Accompanying the image, she wrote a caption that radiated her enthusiasm, “ONE FREAKING WEEK Y’ALL,” and she made sure to tag NBC’s The Voice.

Claire Heilig announced her participation in ‘The Voice.’ (Source: Instagram)

The Bachelor Nation has also officially confirmed Heilig’s return to television, highlighting her previous appearance on The Bachelor, where she didn’t find love, and now she’s set to star in a distinct reality show.

Moreover, Heilig’s passion for music has been with her since a young age, and she has pursued it actively. She has even released four singles since 2021. Her very first release was a cover of Zach Bryan’s ‘Something in Orange,’ which has already been streamed over 1.2 million times on Spotify.

She also dropped three more songs, ‘Chasin’ You’ in 2022, ‘Cover Me Up’ in 2023 (both Morgan Wallen covers), and her most recent single, ‘What Do You Wear to a Breakup,’ written by Catherine McGrath.

Given her established singing talent, Heilig is set to make a significant impact on The Voice show. However, it’s important to note that this won’t be her first time on television, as she previously appeared on Season 26 of The Bachelor.

Claire Heilig’s brief appearance in ‘The Bachelor’

Heilig joined The Bachelor in January 2022. During her time on the show, she garnered attention right from the start, particularly during the show’s premiere, where she stood out for her unfiltered personality.

The television personality quickly determined she wasn’t interested in pursuing a romantic connection with Clayton Echard. Subsequently, she faced elimination in the first week, ultimately not finding her life partner during her time on the show.

Claire Heilig’s love life after her ‘Bachelor Nation’ journey

As of this writing, Heilig appears to be currently single. A look at her Instagram reveals a lack of pictures indicating a romantic relationship.

However, what is abundantly clear from her profile is that she is a devoted single mother to her son. She frequently shares heartwarming photos of herself with her child on Instagram.

For instance, on September 6, 2023, the television personality posted a picture with her son, who was dressed in a school uniform. She captioned the image with, “Officially a second grader,” accompanied by a white heart emoji.

Likewise, it appears that Heilig had a partner who tragically passed away. On August 7, 2023, she posted a poignant video, a compilation of cherished moments she had shared with her boyfriend.

In the accompanying caption, she openly expressed the depth of her emotions, struggling to come to terms with the loss of her partner. She took comfort in the belief that he had found peace.

The television personality continued by saying that she would hold dear all the moments they had shared and find strength in their shared memories. She also expressed a sense of regret, wishing she could have done more.

“Thank you for teaching me to be more understanding, patient, and kind. Until we meet again, rest easy, knowing you are forever loved and missed,” she concluded the post with heartfelt words.
