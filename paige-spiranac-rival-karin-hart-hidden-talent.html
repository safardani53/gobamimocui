KARIN Hart has shown off a hidden talent for her Instagram fans to welcome in 2024.

The self-proclaimed "golf babe," 34, rivals the likes of Paige Spiranac with her glamorous social media content.

Wearing a frilly leather skirt, a short sheer top, and her golf shoes, Hart used a club to open the bottle.

She subsequently celebrated with a swig of bubbly before teeing off.
