THE train collision that happened in the Fisherton Tunnel near Salisbury on Sunday, October 31, 2021, has left the railway out of action.

A carriage was derailed when it hit an object on the line and a second train then crashed into it.

Are SWR trains running between Salisbury and London Waterloo?

All trains between Salisbury and London Waterloo have been cancelled on Monday, November 1, 2021.

The Great Western Rail service from Portsmouth Harbour to Bristol Temple Meads struck a mystery object on Sunday, October 31, 2021 - causing the rear carriage to derail.

The 17:20 South West Rail service from London Waterloo to Honiton in Devon then sped through the tunnel and crashed into the other train leaving dozens of passengers injured as sparks flew.
