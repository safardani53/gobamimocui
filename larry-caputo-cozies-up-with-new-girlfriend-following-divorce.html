Larry Caputo Cozies Up With New Girlfriend Following Divorce

January 2024 · 3 minute read

Fans have been captivated by Theresa Caputo’s ability to speak with the dead for years thanks to TLC’s Long Island Medium. 

But it wasn’t just her special gift that kept them coming back season after season.

Viewers became invested in the Caputo’s family life, including husband Larry and their two children. 

Theresa and Larry were married for over two decades and fans were shocked in 2017 when the couple called it quits.

Even making light jokes that she "should have seen it coming."

But now, Larry Caputo appears to have officially moved on following the divorce. 

The reality star got fans chatting on Thursday afternoon after he took to Instagram to share a photo with his new girlfriend. 

"Love my baby boo!" he captioned the shot which included a heart emoji. 

One follower was supportive commenting, "so happy you’re happy," to which Larry responded, "Thank you so much."

But as to be expected, not everyone was so kind on the social media platform.

"You should go back to Teresa," one user clapped. "It’s a shame to throw 28 years of marriage." 

Larry replied to the hater, "What’s the matter? You got nothing better to do? You must be miserable." 

Back in July 2018, TMZ reported that Larry was seeing a woman named Connie Stauddy after they were spotted shopping together in Alabama.

Connie allegedly was also going through a divorce which I’m sure helped spark up some conversation material between the two. 

Sources told TMZ that Larry and Connie first met when Connie reached out to Theresa for a reading. 

But after Larry’s innocent Instagram post, fans are beginning to miss him and Theresa together.

The Long Island medium reassures us that she is more than ok and is spending her time focusing on family and work. 

In addition, she is traveling across the country for the Theresa Caputo live tour and the star is expected to return for another season on TLC.

And when it comes to her ex-husband?

"There is no purpose in being angry and being mad," she shared with E! News.

"We have over 30 years together and you just can’t forget about that. You know we still have to honor and treasure that and more importantly, respect that."

Besides, the two will be spending a lot of time together as they prepare to marry their only daughter, Victoria Caputo, who recently got engaged.

"Victoria’s ENGAGED! Congratulations to my beautiful baby and her future husband Michael," Theresa shared on Instagram.

"Let the planning begin! #engaged #bridetobe #gettingmarried #longislandmedium."

Larry later joined in on the celebratory posts sharing, "I’m a happy daddy! My baby girl got engaged. I love you soo much Victoria and happy for you and Michael. Let me stop before I start to cry."

And while fans may not be entirely thrilled that the family is going through some dramatic changes, we can’t deny that they seem happy and content with their choice. 

People released a joint statement from the former pair that read, "After 28 years of marriage, we have decided to legally separate. We will always love each other and our two wonderful children."

"We are united in supporting each other and our family."

The two remain friends, even though they are living on opposite costs and focusing on moving forward with their own lives.

And while Theresa may be single as of now, she jokes to E! about her future. 

"Let me finish getting divorced and then you can look for me on Tinder. How’s that?"
