Evidence He Cheated on Kylie Jenner REVEALED?!

August 2024 · 2 minute read

As you’ve likely heard by now, Kylie Jenner has accused Travis Scott of cheating on her.

In case you’ve somehow avoided the whole tawdry situation, here’s a brief rundown:

Travis has spent the past several weeks on tour, and he made the trip from the East Coast to LA in order to surprise Kylie and the couple’s daughter, Stormi.

Unfortunately, the trip went sour in a hurry when — according to TMZ — Kylie discovered "evidence" that Scott had cheated on her.

It first it was unclear exactly what that evidence consisted of.

Now, Radar Online claims to have uncovered the truth about Kylie’s "proof."

And if her case is as flimsy as the site says, she may end up owing Travis an apology.

A source tells Radar that Kylie became angry after receiving a tip from a woman who claimed to have slept with Travis.

“Kylie got anonymous messages from a woman claiming to be Travis’ side piece,” the insider says.

“The woman had no proof, but Kylie flipped out.”

From there we pretty much know what transpired.

Kylie "flipped out" to such a degree that Scott was forced to cancel a scheduled show in Buffalo on Thursday night.

Scott blamed the cancelation on an unspecified illness, but insiders have confirmed that he remained in SoCal in hopes of playing damage control and making things right with Kylie.

Since news of the infidelity accusation broke last night, Scott has maintained his innocence.

"Travis Scott vehemently denies he cheated on Kylie. It is not true. He did not cheat," his rep stated.

"He cancelled one show tonight because he is under the weather."

Insiders say Kylie might not be so quick to believe an anonymous tipster, were it not for the fact that she’s been "paranoid" in recent weeks due to the Tristan Thompson-Jordyn Woods cheating scandal.

Jordyn was Kylie’s best friend, and the fact that she slept with Khloe Kardashian’s boyfriend with seemingly no concern for the damage that would be caused by her actions has left Kylie feeling that she has no one to trust.

But if Travis is telling the truth (and if no further evidence emerges) then we’re sure Kylie will eventually come around.

Until then, dude might do well to remain on his best behavior.
