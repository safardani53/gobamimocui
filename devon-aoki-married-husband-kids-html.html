Devon Aoki Married Life Glimpses With Husband &amp; Kids - Family Goals!

A stylish icon of the fashion scene in the 90’s -  Devon Aoki is an American actress and model who is best known for her supporting roles in popular movies like 2 Fast 2 Furious (2003) and Sin City (2005).

Her other acting credits include lead roles in DOA: Dead or Alive (2006) and Mutant Chronicles (2008).

She has modeled and been featured for brands like Chanel, Hugo Boss, Versace, Balenciaga, Lancome, Oscar de la Renta, and Tiffany & Co. She has also worked as the cover girl for magazines like Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, Nylon, i-D etc.

Devon Aoki Is The Half-Sister Of Famous EDM Artist Steve Aoki

The New York native Devon Edwenna Aoki was born on 10 August 1982 to parents; father Rocky Aoki (former Olympic Wrestler, and founder of American restaurant Benihana) and mother Pamela Hilburger (a jewelry designer).

Find Out The Massive Net Worth Amassed By Devon Aoki As Model And Actress

The gorgeous lady with a height of 5’5 inches started her modeling career at an early age of 13 after she was introduced to the supermodel Kate Moss. She got discovered at a concert in NYC and was signed to an agency a years later.

After making her runaway debut in 1997, she ramp-walked for prestigious brands like Balenciaga, Chanel, and Comm des Garcons. A year later just at the age of 16, she became the face of Versace replacing Naomi Campbell.

Over the years she reached peaks in her career. She has been featured in promotional campaigns for brands like Yves Saint Laurent, Chanel, Versace, Hugo, Lo’real, Lancome etc. She has also walked for designers like Diane Von, Celine, Baby Phat, Marc Jacobs etc.

Later in her career, she also bloomed as an actress and appeared in several movies, the latest one being Double Duchess: Seeing Double. She also appeared in music videos like Electric Barbarella by Duran Duran, M.I.L.F by Fergie, Waste It On Me by her brother Steve Aoki ft. BTS.

With such a successful career as a model and actress, she has accumulated a massive net worth of $20 million.
