THE top 10 sexiest sportsmen on the planet have been decided.

The list features heavyweight boxers, footballing royalty and champion motorsport drivers.

The data compiled by Illicitencounters.com saw 2,000 UK-based women asked to rate 30 famous and active male sports stars on a 1-10 scale in terms of their "sexiness" or "affair factor".

But who comes out on top?

10. Conor McGregor

IRISH MMA fighter Conor McGregor ranks in at number ten on this list.

The Notorious, who has been sidelined since 2021 since breaking his leg, confirmed his return to UFC would occur next year, possibly at UFC 300.

NOT to be outdone, Man City ace Erling Haaland ranks number six on the list.

PERHAPS boosted by his newfound love with musician Taylor Swift, NFL star Travis Kelce kick starts the top three for this list.
