Is The Watcher on Netflix based on a true story?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

NEW Netflix series The Watcher follows the story of Derek and Maria Broaddus and their creepy experience moving home.

But is the drama based on real life experiences? Here's everything we know about the terrifying story.

Is The Watcher based on a true story?

Yes, The Watcher is based on a true story.

Derek and Maria Broaddus were subjected to a creepy experience  after they bought their dream home in Westfield, New Jersey, in 2014.

They received a series of creepy letters from an anonymous weirdo, known only as "The Watcher".

The family were preparing to move into their new home when the letters began to arrive.

Across the next few years the notes would become increasingly threatening and even appearing to threaten their children.

What is The Watcher about?

The Watcher tells the story of the family's ordeal between 2014 and when they sold the house in 2019.

Fictionalised version of the couple will be played by Naomi Watts and Bobby Cannavale.

The first letter arrived just three days after the family moved in.

The letter began: “Dearest new neighbour....allow me to welcome you to the neighbourhood.

It then continued to reveal that the Dutch colonial-style home had been stalked for DECADES.

It read: "657 Boulevard has been the subject of my family for decades now and as it approaches its 110th birthday, I have been put in charge of watching and waiting for its second coming.

"My grandfather watched the house in the 1920s and my father watched in the 1960s. It is now my time."

Other letters included threats to the family and seemed to express anger over the refurbishment of the house.

The Watcher identified Derek and Maria’s children by their birth order and, most worryingly, their nicknames.

The Netflix series will show the ordeal through the years and the toll it took on the family.

How can I watch The Watcher?

The Watcher is a Netflix original series and will be available on that platform around the world.

It was released in full on October 13, 2022.

There are seven episodes, each about 50 minutes long.

Netflix is available for $9.99 per month in the US for a basic membership.
