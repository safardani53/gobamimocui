Antonia's social media slide show was merely to exhibit how radiant she looked in her pale pink bandeau mini-dress.

Her golden tan contrasted gorgeously with her pastel-colored ensemble.

Melissa shares Antonia and two sons with her husband of 16 years, Joe.
