Teen Mom fans spot 'hilarious' detail about Amber Portwood's ex-fianc Matt Baier in resurfaced cli

July 2024 · 4 minute read

TEEN Mom fans have spotted a hilarious detail about Amber Portwood’s ex-fiancé Matt Baier in a resurfaced clip from the MTV show.

Fans rushed to Reddit to discuss the observation in a new thread.

Matt, 49, appeared on numerous episodes of Teen Mom OG during his relationship with Amber, 31.

The pair were together from 2013 until 2017.

Despite calling it quits, MTV fans noticed Amber kept a part of their relationship close to home in a resurfaced clip from the popular series.

In the thread, one viewer shared a photo of Amber outside of her home.

While Amber had moved on with her now-ex Andrew Glennon by that point and was pregnant with his child, she was still using a doormat that read: “Mr. and Mrs. Baier.”

FANS REACT

Alongside the snap, the fan wrote: “Doing a rewatch of season 7 and Amber is using a Mr./Mrs. Baier doormat while pregnant with James and living with Andrew, WTF.”

Another person pointed out: “And it really just says Amber and Matt in different fonts all over it.”

Teen Mom viewers continued to share their thoughts on the decoration, with someone else writing: “That’s pretty funny. Certainly they saw that all the time.”

An additional person shared their theory on why Amber kept the doormat, writing: “Maybe Amber liked the sentiment of wiping her feet on that dead a** s**t relationship and the fact she was pregnant with new boyfriend's baby (who also wiped feet on said mat) was probably a massive F U to Matt? Just conspiring.”

"She probably thought it was therapeutic stepping on it," a following person chimed in.

Someone else wrote: "Wow that is major cringe .... even more so that she was with Andrew at the time. Maybe she liked trampling on it?"

FAILED ROMANCE

The exes relationship issues began when Amber had Matt take a polygraph test following rumors that he tried to have sex with another woman. 

The polygraph test claimed Matt made "sexual advances” to another woman, which caused Amber to leave the room and break down in tears. 

Amber and Matt appeared on Marriage Boot Camp: Family Edition, which aired in 2018, in an attempt to salvage their relationship, though the pair ultimately split. 

Matt moved out of their Indiana home and moved to Las Vegas, where he currently lives today. 

Back in January 2021, The Sun exclusively revealed that Matt dropped 60lbs after changing his diet and exercising.

Following her romance with Matt, Amber moved on with Andrew, 37, and they welcomed their now 3-year-old son James.

However, their romance didn't last and the exes have been feuding over custody for years.

SUPPORTING GARY

In addition to James, Amber shares 13-year-old daughter Leah with ex Gary Shirley, 35.

While the co-parents haven't always seen eye to eye, Amber and Gary now appear to be on better terms.

Earlier this month, Amber showed her support for Gary after he underwent carpal tunnel surgery.

His wife, Kristina, recently updated fans, telling them the surgery went well and he is "back home and resting."

This led Amber to show her support for her ex on Instagram, writing: "Sending love to everyone today. Sometimes we all need a little extra help and love.

"I ask of all my beautiful souls to please pray or meditate or do a mantra..it doesn't matter what your beautiful religion is or if you may have other beliefs.

"Please send all of your love positivity and encouraging words for me, my family, and especially @itsgarytime @kristina_shirley3 for his recovery from bilateral carpal tunnel surgery."
