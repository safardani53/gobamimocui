Early Life, Family & Education

Derek Luke was born on April 24, 1974, in Jersey City, New Jersey, the United States of America, to parents Maurice Luke and Marjorie Dixon.

His father, who is from Georgetown, Guyana, is also an actor while his mother is a pianist. He was raised along with his two siblings; brothers Maurice Luke and Daniel Luke.

He is of American nationality and belongs to black ethnicity. His birth sign is Taurus. He studied at Linden High School and then attended Henry Snyder High School from where he graduated in 1993.
