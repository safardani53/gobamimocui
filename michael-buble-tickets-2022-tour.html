Michael Buble tickets ON SALE NOW: 2022 tour dates, locations, prices and where to buy

October 2024 · 3 minute read

MICHAEL Buble has announced a huge tour for next July and it's a must see for fans of the swing sensation.

He's rescheduled his tour to Summer 2022, and this is everything you need to know if you want tickets.

The singer has rescheduled his An Evening With Michael Buble tour for next summer, and he's added two new dates.

He's added two new shows, one at Aylesbury's Waddesdon Manor on July 2, and the second at Kelso's Floors Castle on July 4.

All dates on the 2021 tour have been rescheduled to 2022, and i f you've already got tickets to the 2021 tour, good news your tickets will remain valid.

If you haven't, or fancy going to see the Canadian crooner then tickets will go on sale December 3 at 9am.

Tickets will be available from AXS and Ticketmaster here.

Michael Buble 2022 UK Tour Dates

An Evening with Micahel Buble will see Michael perform some of his most popular hits, as well as classics from the Rat Pack Era, and new tracks from tenth album, Love.

The shows will see him perform " series of tour dates that will visit picturesque stately homes, castles and cricket grounds across the country" - and can you imagine a better backdrop?

Where can I buy tickets for An Evening with Michael Buble?

Tickets are available from Ticketmaster and AXS here.

They went on sale at 9am on December 3 - but don't hang about if you're interested in seeing him: we think these will go quickly.

How much are tickets for An Evening with Michael Buble?

According to AXS, tickets prices start at £70 and range to £175.

Premium and VIP options will differ according to venue.

Is Michael Buble still touring in 2021?

Sadly, the tour was originally planned for summer 2020 and 2021, but had to be postponed due to COVID-19 restrictions.

New rescheduled dates have been announced for summer 2022.
