When is the All Saints 2018 tour? Tickets, UK dates, venues and prices

April 2024 · 2 minute read

The 90s pop group, who reformed in 2016, have released 13 new tracks for Testament.

Here’s everything to know about when they’re playing and how to get tickets…

What are the All Saints 2018 tour dates?

Nicole, Natalie, Shaznay and Melanie will be performing throughout November and December:

  • September 26 2018, Komeda Brighton
  • November 29 2018, Corn Exchange Cambridge
  • November 30 2018, O2 Academy Oxford
  • December 1 2018, UEA Norwich
  • December 3 2018, O2 Institute Birmingham
  • December 4 2018, Rock City Nottingham
  • December 6 2018, Eventim Apollo London
  • December 7 2018, Academy Bournemouth
  • December 8 2018, Academy Manchester
  • December 10 2018, O2 Academy Glasgow
  • December 11 2018, O2 Academy Leeds
  • December 12 2018, O2 Academy Newcastle-under-Lyme

When do All Saints tickets go on sale? How can I get them?

Tickets for the TESTAMENT Tour are on sale now.

You can buy them from the band’s official website, Ticketmaster, Live Nation and See Tickets.

Prices range from £32.95 for a regular ticket, to £105 for the VIP package which includes early entry to the venue, souvenirs and the chance to have a picture taken with the band.
