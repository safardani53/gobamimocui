KOURTNEY Kardashian's rebel son Reign has interrupted her tour of her $9m Calabasas mansion's backyard.

The Hulu star's seven-year-old son was captured screaming an NSFW word in the new video.

Kourtney, 43, shared videos and photos of inside and outside her house in a home video on her Instagram Stories Monday.

After posts that featured her sister Kendall Jenner's 818 Tequila brand's mobile bar parked in the driveway and catering prepared by the Kardashian family favorite Chef K, the Poosh founder brought fans into her backyard.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum was photobombed by her husband Travis Barker's 19-year-old son, Landon, before she was able to record the sun setting into the hills behind her home.

As she walked along the edge of her pool, Kourtney briefly captured her youngest son, Reign, jumping on the trampoline with a friend.

OUT OF HAND

While fans considered the interruption cute they were less impressed last month after photos showed Reign giving an NSFW gesture.

Reign is flipping off the camera in both photos.

A fourth fan raged: "Personally I love and adore the Kardashians... but Kourtney's children are honestly so spoilt and aren’t disciplined at all!"

They continued, "Kourtney, this isn’t how to raise your kids. It’s absolutely bad parenting."

"... How about teaching these kids some manners and respect?" questioned another.

CUTTING UP

Recently, Reign seemed to misbehave while at a restaurant opening by making the same shocking gesture to the cameras.

Reign appeared to have had enough with photographers at the Los Angeles event.

The little one accompanied his mother and step-father to the grand opening of the vegan eatery, Crossroads Kitchen in Calabasas, California, on Thursday.

Travis is an investor in the upscale restaurant owned by legendary chef Tal Ronnen, who also has a location in West Hollywood.

The Blink-182 drummer shared photos on his Instagram Stories of the ribbon-cutting ceremony, where Reign stood at the front of the unveiling.

In the snaps, Kourtney and Travis watched Reign cut into the ribbon with scissors to signify the restaurant's official opening.

However, one photo circulated capturing a different portrayal of the evening from Reign's eyes.

In the pic, Reign and his sister Penelope were standing in the restaurant's patio area while Travis was talking to a woman seemingly involved in the project.
