Diego Boneta Ariana Grande Dating? Bonding On Scream Queens Set Hollywood Life

October 2024 · 2 minute read

When Diego was asked about his relationship with Ariana in regards to Scream Queens, he couldn’t have been more positive. “I’m going to tell you that I love that we’re together in the series, and I’ll leave it at that,” the hunk told The Latin Post.

It sounds like we’ll definitely see Diego and Ariana on screen together, though their roles are being kept tightly under wraps! Prior to Scream Queens, Diego played Rocco in the Mexican soap opera Rebelde, starred as Javier on 90210, and even had a guest-starring role on hit series Pretty Little Liars. With an impressive resume under his belt, we can’t wait to see who he plays in Scream Queens and what his role will be!

It’s great to see that Ariana has been bonding with her Scream Queens cast. They’ve been like a second family to her! HollywoodLife.com has told you that she has been bonding with co-stars Emma Roberts and Lea Michele, and it looks like Diego has been another one of her behind-the-scenes pals!

‘Scream Queens’ Official Trailer

There’s a whole lot to look forward to on Scream Queens. Between the fabulous cast, the terrifying plot line of a serial killer roaming the campus, and of course, a ton of comedic twists, we have a good feeling Scream Queens will be the new “it” series this fall!
