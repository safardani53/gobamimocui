Who is James Patterson?

James Patterson was born on March 22, 1947, in Newburgh, New York.

James is a best-selling author and philanthropist who is known for writing non-fiction, thrillers, romance novels, fiction, mystery, and other types of books.

Among his notable works are Alex Cross, Women's Murder Club, Michael Bennett, Daniel X, Witch and Wizard, and several notable books.

Before his pursuing a full-time career as an author, he worked in an advertising agency called J. Walter Thompson.
