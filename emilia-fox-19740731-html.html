Emilia Rose Elizabeth Fox is an English actress and host whose film debut was in Roman Polanski's The Pianist. Her other notable films include the Italian-French-British romance The Soul Keeper, for which she won the Flaiano Film Award for Best Actress; drama Love Republic; comedy - drama Things to do before turning 30; the black comedy Keep Mom; the comedy - drama Cashback; drama Retrospective about a fool; the dramatic film Ways to live Forever; thriller A Thousand Kisses Deep; and the horror-thriller Dorian Gray. Fox roles include BBC television series Pride and Prejudice, British / German PBS television series Rebecca VIII, ITV Granada's Henry VIII, BBC Gunpowder, Treason & Plot, BBC miniseries The Virgin Queen from 2005, and ITV crime series Fallen Angel. She also appeared as Morgause in BBC's Merlin, starting with the show's second series. Fox also starred in Delicious.
