Bachelors Erica Rose, Husband Charles Sanders Expecting First Child

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Baby on board! The Bachelor alum Erica Rose Sanders and husband Charles Sanders are expecting their first child together.

“So excited to announce that we we will soon be a family of four! Baby Sanders coming this August,” the 34-year-old reality star wrote via Instagram on Thursday, March 22, alongside a photo of herself with Charles and her daughter from a previous relationship, Holland, 1. “#bigsis #twoundertwo #familyoffour.”

The former ABC personality and the Texas-based investor tied the knot in December, two months after getting engaged. Erica revealed her wedding details in an exclusive interview with Us Weekly before the happy occasion. “Charles and I are both obsessed with the ‘90s so we want our theme to be ‘90s for the party,” she dished. “I’m going to wear a tiara for the wedding and Charles wants to wear a crown!”

Erica and Charles met in high school and reunited years later while she was pregnant with Holland, who she shares with ex-boyfriend Galen Gentry. The Bachelor Pad star and Gentry split shortly after welcoming Holland.

While chatting with Us, Erica said she knew Charles was “the one” for her when the couple were vacationing in Aspen, Colorado, with her little one in August 2017. “Holland was having altitude sickness and he took care of her,” she gushed. “I never told a guy I loved him first in my entire life, but I said it then. We were going to bed and I told Holland I loved her and then I said to Charles, ‘I love you, too.’ And he said it back.”
