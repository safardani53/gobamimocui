Bachelor In Paradise wasnt ready for Becca Serranos sudden exit Fri 10 November 202

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Brunette bombshell Becca Serrano had a brief spell on The Bachelor season 27. After leaving the show before any dating really began, she opted for a second shot at love on Bachelor In Paradise this year. However, after Becca makes an exit without warning, fans are totally confused and others are seeking “closure.”

Bachelor In Paradise begins with a group of original cast members who have already attempted to find romance. Many have looked for love on The Bachelor and The Bachelorette. Becca is a former star of Zach Shallcross‘ season. Dubbed “the prettiest” person in Paradise, she certainly made a grand entrance, but Becca’s time on the ABC show was unusually brief.

Why did Becca leave Bachelor In Paradise?

Becca left Bachelor In Paradise because she had concerns over whether what she had with Brayden Bowers was “real.”

Speaking on the ABC show, Becca explained she was feeling doubtful about their relationship: “I want to see if what we have is real, but I just am afraid… what if it’s not?”

During season 9 episode 7, the men in Paradise held the power in the Rose Ceremony.

Becca was given the option to partake in the Rose Ceremony or go home, and she chose to go home.

Fans rooted for her and Brayden

After arriving in Paradise in episode 6, Becca’s time in Mexico was super brief as she opted to leave in episode 7.

Despite only being on the show for a short time, she and Brayden formed a connection. Their feelings for one another were so strong that Brayden left Rachel Recchia in order to pursue Becca.

Some fans appeared to support Becca and Brayden’s romance, however many were hugely disappointed that Rachel was left in the dust.

Lots of viewers took to X (Twitter) to aur their thoughts about how Rachel “deserves better.”

Becca sparks Bachelor In Paradise fan theory

Since Becca’s mystery exit left fans totally confused, some have come up with their own theories on why she departed from the show.

Some believe that Becca agreed with producers to leave season 9. One person wrote on her Instagram post about her leaving: “Producer asked her to so Brayden can picked Olivia.”

Another commented on the same post: “I know you got set up and was told to leave I know the truth.”

More said: “There weren’t ready for you.”

However, some people had less controversial theories and said that maybe Bachelor star Becca just enjoys a vacation. They tweeted: “So Becca left night one on Zach’s season to leave night one on Paradise?! Maybe she just likes to travel.”
