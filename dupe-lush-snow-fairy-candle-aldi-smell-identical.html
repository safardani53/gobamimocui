I found a cheap 4 dupe for Lushs 36 Snow Fairy candle at Aldi - it smells almost identical

February 2024 · 3 minute read

IF you are a fan of Lush’s Snow Fairy scent, you may wish to head to Aldi.

A shopper has shared how the supermarket is selling a candle that smells nearly identical to the popular candy floss scented Snow Fairy.

In a clip on her @oliviavcox account, the Aldi fan wrote: “POV you run to Aldi to find the Snow Fairy candle dupe.”

Lush’s Snow Fairy range has boomed in popularity over the years, and they’ve expanded their collection to include a candle scent too.

While this will set you back £36 for the four-wick product, the Aldi one is more affordable at £4.99.

Olivia raved about the Sleeps Till Santa candle, from their Christmas Nostalgia Candle range, smelling “incredible” and making her whole room “smell like Snow Fairy.”

“Hundreds and thousands of bubbles from Snow Fairy Roll bubble bar, and a comforting, buttery blanket of Butterbear bath bomb.”

The £13 Little Snow Fairy gift set also has two goodies inside the cardboard box. 

“When you need just a hint of sparkle and sweetness in your life at this time of year, Little Snow Fairy's here,” a blunt on it reads.
