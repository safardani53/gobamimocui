Consider These Factors and Features Before Making a Purchase

There are several factors to consider before purchasing an infinity scarf. Continue reading to see what we suggest.

Color

Infinity scarves come in a wide array of colors. To choose the best color for you, consider your skin tone and hair color. If you have cool undertones, choose a blue, white, grey, or purple scarf. If you have warm skin tones, opt for orange, brown, or red tones. You may also like a cream or beige scarf.

Both types of skin tones suit olive green or plum. You should also consider the color of your coat before buying a scarf. You could wear contrasting colors for a bold statement like red and black, purple and green, or orange and pink.

Alternatively, choose a color that compliments your coat by choosing a darker or paler version of the same color. For example, you could wear a brown coat and a beige scarf, or wear a black coat and a grey scarf.

Material

Infinity scarves are made from a variety of materials. Some of these include:

  • Soft acrylic: Soft acrylic is a popular material used in scarf making. When appropriately treated, it can be as soft as cashmere but much more durable.
  • Wool: There are several types of wool, but they mostly have the same properties. Wool is a great material to keep you warm during winter.
  • Pashmina: Pashmina is high-quality wool produced by goats. The shawl made from this material is also called pashmina.
  • Cashmere: Similarly, cashmere can be made from the hair of pashmina goats, cashmere goats, and other breeds of goat.
  • Faux fur: Faux fur is fluffy and warm. It is made to look like real fur but is much cheaper and suitable for vegans.
  • Faux sheepskin: Faux sheepskin is warm and has a suede-like texture. Often an infinity scarf will be lined with this material rather than be made entirely from it.
  • Polyester: Polyester is often used for cooler, more decorative scarves and holds colors well. The design of a polyester scarf is usually bright and vibrant. When used as an infinity scarf, it may be used in the winter because the scarf’s design requires you to layer the material. In addition, polyester is durable and fast drying.

Cowl scarf and snood

If you want a short infinity scarf, look for a cowl scarf. This scarf usually goes over your head loosely and doesn’t need to be wrapped around to fit. The snood is similar to a cowl, except it also has a hood. 

Style

There are many different types of patterns used for the material of a scarf. The list of available patterns is as long as your imagination, so we’ll just list a few patterns that are in trend right now:

  • Tartan
  • Animal print
  • Floral
  • Aztec
  • Polka dots
  • Stripes
  • Checked
  • Rainbow

Types of knits

If you know you want a knitted scarf but aren’t sure what type of knitted style you want, check out the following knits. Once you find the one you envisioned, you can then search for that knit in the particular color and length you want.

  • Cable knit
  • Ribbed knit
  • Chunky knit
  • Soft knit
  • Confetti knit
  • Drop stitch

Crochet

Crochet is technically different from knitting, although it uses similar wool or wool-like thread. It often has more gaps between the stitches, creating a unique look different from a knitted scarf.

Fleece on the inside

If you want an extra warm winter infinity scarf, look for one with fleece material on the inside. This allows you to keep bright prints like the kind you get on polyester but with added warmth.

Shimmering thread

A shimmering thread throughout various materials is in trend right now and will add some sparkle to your wardrobe. In addition, you can match the metallic thread with your silver or gold jewelry.

Customer reviews

To get the best possible product, you should read the customer reviews of the item you wish to buy. This will let you know whether the scarf is similar to its online product description. Choose a product with mostly positive reviews.

Benefits of Purchasing an Infinity Scarf Online

There are several benefits to buying a scarf online. These include:

  • Saving time: You can look at ten products online in the same amount of time it would take you to look at one product in a store. You also save time traveling to and from the shop and waiting in line.
  • Less physical energy: Shopping from the comfort of your own home is much less physically strenuous than traipsing around shops. You also don’t need to carry any bags online, as you can get the scarf delivered straight to your home.
  • Money-saving options: Online shopping is a very competitive market which means it is where you will find the most bargains. Online retailers can often afford to sell items cheaply due to having no physical location to fund.
  • More choices: Buying online means you get to view way more products than you would in a physical store, and finding them is much easier thanks to search engines and shopping platforms like Amazon.
