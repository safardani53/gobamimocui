Khloe Kardashian slammed for making 'disgusting' comment about daughter True, 4, in new photo

August 2024 · 3 minute read

KHLOE Kardashian has been bashed for her remark about her daughter True's looks in a new photo.

The Kardashians star took to Instagram on Friday to share snaps from a past photoshoot.

In the pic, Khloe, 38, posed donning a black and white body-hugging long-sleeved dress with a stars and stripes design.

She styled her long brunette locks in a half ponytail and accessorized with thick gold hoop earrings.

In one, she stood in front of a white wall, and others alongside a lit-up neon pink sign.

Khloe captioned the post: "Many Moons Ago" with a moon emoji.

Fans flooded the comments section to gush over the reality star's appearance in the shoot.

And some couldn't help but point out the resemblance to her four-year-old daughter.

One particular remark caught Khloe's eye, prompting the TV star to respond.

The fan wrote: "The last picture is all True," with a red heart emoji, and Khloe replied: "awwwew I pray," again adding a red heart emoji.

FANS' REACTIONS

However, some fans weren't pleased with the mom of two's comment and aired their thoughts on an online forum.

One user started the conversation by sharing a screenshot of the exchange with the caption: "Khloe’s response just doesn’t sit right with me."

Others agreed, claiming the star's looks have "changed so much" since undergoing her rumored cosmetic procedures that her daughter wouldn't look like her.

A third ranted: "It's a disgusting response. She could have liked it and moved on at the very least. True won't possibly look like she looks now bc Khloe doesn't look like how she would naturally look anyway."

And a fourth questioned: "Is she trying to say, in a weird and stupid way, that she wishes she looked like True?"

Khloe shares True and her seven-month-old son with her ex-Tristan Thompson, 31.

MORE THROWBACKS

The Hulu personality has repeatedly been accused of going under the knife due to her seemingly altered appearance over the years.

Earlier this week, fans were convinced she got lip fillers after she showed off what looked like a plumper pout in a video with True.

Kar-Jenner fanatics also recently reminisced over her previous looks after someone shared a slew of snaps of Khloe taken in the 1990s and 2000s.

The photos highlighted many eras of the KUWTK alum's style, with her long brunette locks and heavy eye makeup.

Some were solo shots of Khloe posing during various outings, and others were with family members.

In one, Khloe looked nearly unrecognizable alongside her sister Kourtney, 43, brother Rob, 35, and late father, Robert - whom the siblings recently shared tributes to mark his passing.

And others showcased the Good American founder's tiny waistline with her sister, Kim, 42, and her mother, Kris Jenner, 67.

'SUCH A SHAME'

The original poster included in their caption: "Khloe appreciation post."

Another wrote: "I can’t believe people put her down. She was gorgeous. Her body, her face."

A third commented: "It's such a shame... she was STUNNING. Naturally beautiful face with balanced features, and she carried herself with so much confidence. It makes me so sad that she didn't see it and changed herself so much."

A fourth chimed in: "She definitely looks better with darker hair. She looks so confident in most of these photos before all the fillers, surgery, etc. The irony is that people use cosmetic procedures to help boost their esteem, sometimes it does the opposite."
