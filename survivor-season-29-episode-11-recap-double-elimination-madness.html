Survivor Season 29 Episode 11 Recap: Double Elimination Madness!

April 2024 · 3 minute read

On Survivor Season 29 Episode 11, the stakes were higher than ever for the remaining contestants battling for the glory, the bragging rights and the money.

So who outwitted, outplayed and outlasted the competition this week?

Watch Survivor Season 29 Episode 11 Online

When Survivor Season 29 Episode 11 kicked off, it looked like Jon would be blindsided, but Reed’s plan fell apart and Keith’s son Wes ended up booted.

How would Reed respond after coming thisclose to taking control of the game, only to fall short? That was a key storyline of the night on the CBS staple.

The reward challenge was the first individual reward of the fall, and the challenge involved answering trivia questions of sorts about the other castaways.

 

When it boiled down to Jon, Natalie, Jaclyn, Baylor and Missy, it became clear that the alliance had a plan and ultimately, the reward went to Missy.

Things went south, though, after Missy sent Jon to Exile Island and took Baylor and Natalie with her on the reward horseback ride instead of Jaclyn.

On Exile Island, Jon began searching for a new hidden immunity idol, having used his other one in the last tribal council, and once again, he found it.

Missy, Baylor and Natalie began plotting to take their sub-alliance to the final three, while Reed, Alec and Keith were being especially nice to Jaclyn.

Would she flip? And could they bring Jon along once he returned? She saw right through them and was unmoved, but she had no love for Misy either.

Jon returned for the immunity challenge, which Keith was able to take an early lead in and win, his second individual immunity triumph of the season.

Jaclyn and Jon got into a fight after the fact, and Reed approached them both to see if they would be willing to take out Missy. Reed is getting desperate.

It was not clear whom the couple would vote with until the very end, to the point where the two sides resorted to pitching Jon and Jaclyn out in the open.

In the end, they voted off Reed, sticking to their original plans.

At that point, a lonely Alec asked Jon if there was any way to convince him not to vote him out next. Jon declared he’d stick with his current alliance.

At the reward challenge, the castaways had to balance both a ball between a peg and a handle and themselves on a perch to win a canopy bed for a night.

Natalie held on the longest to earn a comfortable night’s sleep.

Alec was sent to Exile Island, while Jon confessed to Natalie, who earned his trust back with the reward, that he had an immunity idol stashed away.

However, she was plotting to blind side him as revenge for Jeremy, approaching Baylor to vote him out along with Alec and Keith behind Missy’s back!

Alec returned for the second immunity challenge, which required the castaways to balance wooden blocks on a tray. Jon won the challenge and with it immunity.

Right before the tribal council, reloaded, Jon decided that Keith should be the next to go from Survivor, splitting votes with Alec in case one had an idol.

Natalie was kinda pissed that she did not have the chance to vote Jon out. Still determined to shake things up, she considered flipping to take out Alec first.

When push came to shove, Jon claimed that Keith needed to go based on his success at immunity challenges, but Natalie flipped, booting Alec at the last minute!
