What did Matty Healy say about Ice Spice?

April 2024 · 2 minute read

SINGER Matty Healy has sparked fury among music fans after he reportedly made derogatory comments about Ice Spice.

The controversial conversation left Healy in an awkward position after his rumoured girlfriend, Taylor Swift, announced a collaboration with the female rapper.

Why are fans mad at Ice Spice?

On May 24, 2023, Taylor Swift revealed she would collaborate with Ice Spice, who will feature on the deluxe version of her new album Midnights.

The announcement sparked controversy because of Taylor's rumoured relationship with 1975 frontman Matty Healy.

Healy has come under fire for comments he made on The Adam Friedland Show Podcast that were directed at the rapper.

Music fans have since hit out on Twitter - attacking Ice Spice for championing the alleged relationship.
