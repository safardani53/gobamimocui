KARDASHIAN fans think Kourtney and Travis Barker have celebrated their FOURTH wedding on Wednesday, June 8, 2022.

New photos show what many called a "creepy and satanic" ceremony filled with blood vials and skulls.

Kourtney Kardashian, 43, and Travis Barker, 46, first shocked fans in April with a post-Grammys wedding ceremony at a chapel in Las Vegas.

However, days later, Kourtney declared it "practice" as no license was involved, so the marriage had not yet become official.

The couple legally tied the knot in an intimate courthouse ceremony held in Santa Barbara, California, last month with only the reality star's grandma and the rocker's dad present.

Nearly the whole Kardashian-Jenner clan flew into Portofino, Italy, to celebrate their third ceremony with extravagant week-long festivities.

Now, she has shared surprise photos from a new event, and fans wonder if it's the FOURTH.

On June 8, 2022, Kourtney shared a slideshow of images from a candle-lit ceremony on her Instagram.

"Romeo and Juliet vibes for the Bach," the reality star captioned it, referencing the Shakespearian lovers who died for each other.

She also shared a heart and a skull emoji.

In the first photo, Kourtney wore a low-cut lace dress with her hair in a bun as she leaned in to kiss her Blink-182 drummer husband.

LOVE BITES

Kourtney has embraced the darker side of things since embarking on her romance with Travis.

Fans have noticed that she now dresses more goth than her previous outfit choices.

For her extravagant wedding reception in Italy, she slipped into a black corset style dress while Travis ripped off her garter with his teeth.

As viewers of The Kardashians on Hulu saw, the pair even wore matching skeletons' onesies for their visit to an IVF clinic.

Whatever they're wearing, the couple is always sure to express their affection for each other.

Kourtney and Travis like to pack on the PDA on the red carpet, on a yacht, and even while viewing houses.
