EDDIE HEARN was left red-faced and bare-bottomed thanks to a classic prank from Tommy Coyle.

The promoter had his pants pulled down by his own fighter — before Coyle shared the hilarious video on Twitter.

Coyle, 29, sneaks up behind Hearn on an escalator at the Bistro Bar in Boston and pulls Eddie’s shorts down, revealing the promoter's rear end, much to the amusement of customers watching nearby.

Hearn will be relieved he was not facing the camera when he was caught out!

And the Hull-born fighter joked he might lose his job after pulling off the stunt on his boss to perfection.

Coyle said: "Billion Dollar Debag. The Boss had to get it. @EddieHearn I’m getting sacked in the morning."
