The Fosters Cierra Ramirezs Ex Boyfriend Subtly Threw Shade at Her after Split

September 2024 Â· 3 minute read

The Fosters' Cierra Ramirez and her ex boyfriend Jeff Wittek, a Vlog Squad member and show host, apparently didn't break up on good terms.   

After their breakup, Ramirez's ex subtly shaded her during an episode of his talk show-style YouTube series, Jeff's Barbershop. 

During September 29, 2020 episode titled 'CONFRONTING BRYCE HALL ABOUT HIS MISTAKES,' Wittek invited bad boy TikToker Bryce Hall. 

Cierra Ramirez's former boyfriend, Jeff Wittek. (Source: Jeff Wittek/Instagram)

"He's really easy to hate. But as a fellow guy with no talent and who's only famous because of his ex-girlfriend, I can relate to Bryce," said Wittek as he introduced his guest. 

Fans realized that Wittek's self-deprecating comment probably stemmed from the fact that many people thought his career boosted after dating Ramirez. 

Ramirez's former boyfriend, however, did not go into details on how she contributed to his success. 

Cierra Ramirez and Ex Boyfriend's Relationship

Ramirez and Wittek first met in a club in Los Angeles and eventually started dating in 2015. 

In March that same year, they made their relationship Instagram official with a series of loved-up snaps. 

These enviable photos documented the times they spent together on romantic dates and casual hangouts with friends. 

Ramirez's ex branched out into acting and landed roles in short comedy films like Declined Credit Card or Crazy Drug Lord, both of which were written and directed by Anwar Jibawi. 

He then appeared in a TV mini-series titled Lele Pons and Hannah Stocking in the same year. 

In 2017, the duo embarked on their first and only project together, a short movie titled Petting Scorpions, written and directed by The Young Astronauts. 

Many people thought part of Wittek's success could be owed to the Good Trouble star, who brought the YouTuber to prominence after they started dating. 

Unfortunately, after three years, they broke up and never shared why. 

Cierra Ramirez and Her Current Boyfriend

Ramirez is currently dating Johnny Zallez, who goes by Othrsyde on Instagram.

Othrsyde, or "other side," admittedly is a musician who aspires to be relatable to his fans. He wants them to know he has gone through everything they have through his music. 

The couple started dating in August 2019, and a year later, Ramirez uploaded a photo with her beau in honor of their first anniversary. 

Ramirez often gives fans a glimpse into their love life with adorable pictures. On December 3, 2020, she opened up about her love for her boyfriend on his birthday. 

The actress uploaded a series of pictures of her and Zallez with a heartfelt caption.

"You ever meet someone & think about how much better life is..simply because they're in it? Thanks for being born @othrsyde, you make everyday feel like a birthday," she wrote.
