Bradley Cooper and Irina Shayk are great co-parents. Check out cute photos of the actor and model with daughter Lea over the years.

Bradley Cooper and Irina Shayk welcomed their first child together, Lea De Seine, on March 21, 2017. The relationship, however, ran its course by 2019, and the couple mutually decided to split. Their breakup was confirmed in June of that year.

Still, the former couple has kept close to make sure daughter Lea has plenty of family time. The duo has proved there is no animosity as they team up for playdates in New York City, where Irina lives, every so often. They began fueling reconciliation rumors in late 2022, after snapping a sultry Halloween photo before spending the winter holidays together.

Bradley Cooper and Irina Shayk were happy as can be during a day with their daughter Lea in New York City on June 1, 2021. They were dressed for a sunny day at the playground, though Irina did it quite fashionably.
