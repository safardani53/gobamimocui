Early Life, Family & Education

Stewart was born on May 22, 2001, in Rochester, New York, as one of seven children to Dela Stewart & Shameka Holloway.

His parents met in New York in the 1990s, where his father worked at a construction site, operating a jackhammer before retiring in October 2019.

Isaiah’s father Dela is a native of Jamaica who emigrated to the United States in the 1970s & worked on a farm in South Flordia before moving to New York.

As reported, the basketball star’s father & mother are separated but are on good terms.

After living as an infant with his mom, then 4-year-old Isaiah moved in permanently with his dad Dela Stewart & older brother Martin.

Isaiah attended McQuaid Jesuit High School in Rochester and later transferred to La Lumiere School during his junior year.

For his collegiate basketball, he enrolled at the University of Washington in 2019. After his freshmen year, Isaiah Stewart declared for the NBA Draft on April 1, 2020.

Isaiah Stewart’s Feud With LeBron James

The NBA player has been making waves with his impressive performances on the court. However, Stewart’s professional career is not immune to controversies though.

On November 21, 2021, he was involved in a scuffle with one of the greatest basketball players of all time, LeBron James, in a 116-121 loss against LA Lakers.

While attempting for the rebound, James’ elbow hit Stewart’s face. Subsequently, the Pistons power forward charged against him and shoved LeBron that resulted in confrontation between the two.

Due to the incident, both Isaiah Stewart & LeBron James were ejected from the game.

While Stewart saw the suspension of two games with a $45,201 fine, while the Lakers star was ruled out for one game.

NBA Career & Achievements

Stewart became the 16th overall pick (first round) of Portland Trail Blazers in the 2020 NBA Draft. He was the fourth power forward pick of the draft, following: Onyeka Okongwu, Obi Toppin & Jalen Smith.

However, shortly after the draft, the Blazers traded him to Houston Rockets, before another trade, to Detroit Pistons in exchange for Christian Wood.

With the Pistons, Isaiah made his NBA debut on December 28, 2020, in a loss against Atlanta Hawks.

Then on April 29, 2021, he scored 20 points (his 2020 season-high) in a game against Dallas Mavericks.

In the 2021-22 NBA season, Isaiah Stewart played in 71 games (started in all) and averaged 8.3 points per game (589 points), 8.7 rebounds per game & 1.2 assists per game.

Net Worth & Salary Of Isaiah Stewart

As of December 2023, Isaiah Stewart has an estimated net worth of $3 million. All this hefty fortune came through his NBA contracts & endorsement earnings.

With the Pistons, Stewart signed a four-year rookie contract worth $15.10 million with the Pistons on November 30, 2020.
