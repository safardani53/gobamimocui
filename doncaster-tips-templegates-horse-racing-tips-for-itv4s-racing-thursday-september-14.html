Doncaster TV tips - Templegate's top horse racing tips for ITV4's racing on Thursday, September 14

June 2024 ¬∑ 3 minute read

I'M loco for Motion in the Group 2 Park Hill Stakes (3.00 Doncaster).

Melodic Motion has done nothing but improve since winning on her reappearance this season.

She coped well with the step up in class to finish an impressive third in a Goodwood Group 3 when things did not go her way and can land back-to-back wins for Ralph Beckett after Simple Verse's success in this race last year.

1.50: I'VE found the mother of all bets in the Carrie Red nursery.

THE MUMS is the least experienced of these but is making swift progress.

She was a game winner at Newbury last time when relishing the soft ground and can improve again with that experience under her belt.

Clubbable has yet to get her head in front but came close at Hamilton last time on her nursery debut.

She should break her duck before long but looks vulnerable to an unexposed improver.

2.25: IT'S May day for TAJAANUS.

Richard Hannon's runner has won three of her four starts, rounding off with victory in a competitive Sweet Solera Stakes at Newmarket's July meeting.

The race has worked out well and Hannon has high hopes for his filly.

Nyaleti did her profile no harm with a decent effort against the colts in the Group 1 Prix Morny at Deauville. Mark Johnston's runners thrive on racing and she is sure to go close.

Billesdon Brook is a solid performer. She has never been out of the first three in seven starts but may lack the class of her Hannon stablemate.

3.00: MELODIC MOTION can call the tune.

The three-year-old has improved all season and was a brave third after being knocked about in the Lillie Langtry at Goodwood.

She copes well with soft ground and looked to relish the step up to this mile and three-quarters at Goodwood.

Natural Scenery is another filly who could have landed a big 'un last time with a change of luck.

She had no sort of run in the Ebor last month and deserves to win a decent prize.

Dubka paid the price for racing prominently in the Ebor. This classy filly is worth another chance.

3.35: HAVE a Power trip in this valuable sales race.

ALBA POWER was not far behind Tangled and Great Prospector in York's sales race last month and faces that pair on better terms.

He can be another big winner for Josephine Gordon who has formed a lucrative partnership with Hugo Palmer.
