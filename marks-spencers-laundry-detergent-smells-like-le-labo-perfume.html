I tried the new Marks &amp; Spencers laundry detergent &amp; it smells exactly like Le Labo perfume - but

June 2024 · 2 minute read

A WOMAN has revealed that the new line of Marks and Spencer's laundry detergent smells exactly like Le Labo perfume.

Laura Capon, who has spent years working as a beauty editor certainly knows a thing or two about perfume.

So she was left stunned when she picked up the laundry detergent and fabric softener and realised it smelt exactly like the pricey Le Labo perfume.

Taking to TikTok, Laura, best know as LauCapon on the app, couldn't wait to share her discovery of the M&S laundry items in the scents Sandalwood and Bergamot and Sandalwood and Rose.

She said: "You can tell this is an urgent video because I've literally propped you up on my dish drainer.

"I was in M&S earlier and their new cleaning range is A) beautiful for a cleaning range."

She added if you like Le Labo perfumes or that warm, 'sandalwoody' scent then their new range is for you.

Not only does it smell warm - but the beauty editor revealed it also smelt super expensive.

Laura continued: "These laundry liquids and fabric conditioners are chef's kiss."

The beauty editor added: "You'll just smell rich. you and your clothes will smell warm and rich."
