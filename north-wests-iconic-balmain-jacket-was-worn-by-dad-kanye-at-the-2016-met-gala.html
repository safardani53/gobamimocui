North Wests iconic Balmain jacket was worn by dad Kanye at the 2016 Met Gala Fri 29 De

June 2024 · 2 minute read

North West‘s Balmain jacket certainly looked familiar, but then it clicked! She donned the same sparkly 2016 outfit worn by her dad, Kanye West, on the Met Gala red carpet. North has always been supportive of her father’s style, and even though the rapper didn’t get an invite to the party, his jacket did…

North West’s Balmain jacket

North West’s jacket from Kanye’s archive is a custom Balmain embellished trucker jacket by Olivier Rousteing. She digged out the 2016 look and smiled for pictures with her mom, Kim Kardashian.

Kim and her daughter posed for photos under their Christmas tree at her Christmas Eve party, held at her home surrounded by celebrity guests and many pine trees covered in fake white snow.

The Hulu star wrote on her Instagram Story, “You know North went through the archives to pick her Dad’s Balmain Met jacket, iykyk [if you know, you know].” And yes, Kim, we do know!

Kanye wore jacket to the Met Gala

The embellished trucker jacket that Kanye wore to the Met Gala was complimented by blue contact lenses and blue ripped jeans. He even matched with his then-wife’s silver costume dress!

Met Gala’s theme for that year was Manus x Machina: Fashion In An Age Of Technology, and the parents both wore blinged out looks for the occasion, while holding hands on the red carpet.

North’s party look hailed ‘iconic’

When North rolled up all smiles to The Kardashian Christmas Eve party wearing the oversized Balmain jacket belonging to her father, fans were ecstatic and hailed the look “cute” and “iconic.”

“Why is no one talking about #NorthWest wearing Ye’s Balmain 2016 Met Gala Jacket?!?!?! This is a beautiful homage to Kanye,” penned a Twitter user.
