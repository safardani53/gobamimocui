Kelly Clarkson flaunts thinner-than-ever frame in red dress on her talk show after sparking concern

March 2024 Â· 3 minute read

KELLY Clarkson has continued to show off her dramatic weight loss after previously sparking concern with her transformation.

The American Idol winner was featured on the Instagram account of her talk show, The Kelly Clarkson Show, on Wednesday.

The post revealed the show's latest guests, Ted Lasso star Hannah Waddingham, 49, and sisters Nikki & Brie Garcia.

In the caption, the show declared that they were "getting in the holiday spirit" as Kelly, 41, was seen posing alongside the women.

The holiday-themed set displayed the show's decorated Christmas tree, garland, and hanging ornaments as the stars stood amongst them.

Kelly was seen rocking a tea-length red dress, which featured multiple bow accents along her midsection.

The Breakaway hitmaker opted to tie her hair back and let her new bangs sweep across her forehead - despite receiving criticism for the haircut in recent weeks.

She paired her look with strappy black heels and stood tall as she showed off her slimmer-than-ever frame.

LOVING THE PROCESS

Kelly has been showing off her weight loss transformation in recent weeks after previously opening up about the criticism she's faced since her time on Season 1 of American Idol back in 2002.

The Miss Independent singer revealed on The Ellen DeGeneres Show in 2015 that people would constantly call her "big" in the past.

After shedding serious weight over the past few months, Kelly revealed that she has "loved" the process.

As the singer spoke with sisters Barbara, 41, and Jenna Bush Hager, 41, on her show, the trio delved into the topic of not fitting into their clothes anymore.

Kelly added: "Ugh, I don't fit into mine. I love losing weight but here's the thing. Jeans are so hard, I feel like when you have a butt and a smaller waist."

She also bragged about not having to wear Spanx since losing weight recently.

On an episode of her talk show, she revealed: "I don't even need to wear Spanx anymore, I just wear them for warmth like thermals."

She added that "life is good" after once more confirming that she no longer has to wear the garment unless she wants to.

FAN CONCERN

Although most of the star's fans have been supportive of her transformation, some have expressed concerns.

On last Thursday's episode of The Kelly Clarkson Show, Kelly wore a floral shirt and a maxi-denim skirt.

On Instagram, Kelly shared photos with that day's guests once more - Sara Bareilles and vegan chef Pinky Cole.

Many of her fans praised her trim figure, but others feared that the singer had lost too much weight.

"Kelly looks really skinny," one person wrote.

"I was thinking the same thing. I agree," a fan agreed with the previous commenter.

"I hope she's doing alright," a third added.

The American Idol alum's weight loss has surely been a hot topic, with rumors swirling about how she managed to slim down.
