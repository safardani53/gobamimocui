When does Eric Nam go on tour?

Eric Nam is a Stone Music Entertainment singer, songwriter, and television personality who was born on November 17, 1988, in Atlanta, Georgia.

He first rose to fame in the early 2010s and was later named GQ Korea's Man of the Year in 2016 and included on Forbes' 30 Under 30 Asia list in 2017.

Thanks to the success of his music, which includes the albums Before We Begin (2019) and There And Back Again (2022), he has become a household name and one of YouTube Music's Global Trending Artists on the Rise.

Fans will be able to see the superstar live in concert starting in September 2023.

When does Eric Nam's album come out?

Nam's upcoming tour is a celebration of his third studio album, House on a Hill.

The album will feature eight songs in the K-Pop genre and is set to be released on September 8, 2023.

How can I get tickets for Eric Nam's tour?

Fans looking to score tickets for Nam's tour can do so in a number of ways.

They can either purchase them directly on his website or on Ticketmaster and Live Nation.

General public sales are set to begin on June 30, 2023, at 10am local time.
