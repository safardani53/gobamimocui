Lele Pons A YouTube Star Got Married To Guaynaa

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Lele Pons, a popular YouTuber, recently married rapper Guaynaa on March 4. The wedding ceremony took place at Miami’s Fairchild Botanical Gardens. Among those in attendance were Camila Cabello, Paris Hilton, Steve Aoki, Juanpa Zurita, Winnie Harlow, Anwar, Julissa Prado, James Charles, Danny Ocean, Chayanne, Kunno, Domelipa, Aitana, and others from the entertainment industry. At their wedding, the couple cut a 10-tier cake while celebrating with close friends and family. Natalia Jimenez performed with a mariachi band at the celebration, followed by Becky G, Sebastian Yatra, Natti Natasha, and others taking the stage.

Guaynaa and Lele Pons met in 2019.

Lele Pons hinted at a 2020 musical collaboration with Guaynna. Se Te Nota, the duo’s debut single, was released in September of the same year, and they began promoting it. Pons and Guaynaa were seen hanging out together and sharing social media posts featuring each other after the success of their single. They even went to parties together, including a Halloween party in October 2020. They then shared a photo of themselves kissing each other in November of the same year and confirmed their relationship the following month.

The couple also went on vacation together, and Guaynaa was frequently seen in Pons’ videos. In March 2021, Lele revealed on social media that they first met in 2019. The couple soon bought a house, and rumors of their engagement spread when they posted photos of a fake proposal in April 2022. The real proposal, however, took place the following year at the Tomorrowland Festival. Lele Pons rose to prominence through her Vine appearances and YouTube comedy sketches. She has appeared in a number of TV shows and films.

Guaynaa appeared as a guest on Becky G’s album.

Guaynaa, who was born on September 16, 1992, has so far released one album, titled La Republica, which will be released in October 2021 by Republic and Universal Latino. In May 2020, he also released an EP titled BRB Be Right Back.

He is best known for his single Rebota, which peaked at number one on the Billboard Hot Latin Songs chart, and he later signed with Universal Music Latino and Republic Records. He also appeared as a guest on Becky G’s album Esquemas. The album, which was released in May 2022, featured 14 singles and debuted at the top of the US Billboard 200, US Top Latin Albums, and US Latin Pop Albums. The RIAA also certified the album platinum. Guaynaa’s singles include Pirateando, La Stripper, Bugalu, Chicharron, Buyaka, Full Moon, Taxi, Mera, Agachaito, Monterrey, Demente, Calle, and others.
