Last week, Sofia stormed off the set of America's Got Talent after she got into a brutal competition with fellow judge Heidi Klum.

The contest was filmed previously, and it was called Name That Toot and was introduced by the host of the show Terry Crews, 55.

Riccardo can create a scarily accurate tune with just his hands by making fart noises.
