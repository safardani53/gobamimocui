The White Board of PAIN

Tuesday’s Dance Moms Season 4 Episode 25 introduced us to "The White Board" and all the tension you knew that would entail on the Lifetime hit.

This isn’t just any old board. Oh no. It is the venerable White Board of PAIN.

Watch Dance Moms Season 4 Episode 25 Online

At the "Sheer Talent" competition in New Jersey, the moms are fuming over the treatment Maddie gets from Abby. What makes Maddie so special?!

The pyramid has Kendall, Chloe and MacKenzie on the bottom, with Nia and Maddie on top to go up against Candy Apples and dreaded foe Cathy.

Gino and Maddie competing against each other? OH it’s ON now. Though Maddie has a part in a web-based program and may miss the competition.

As a result, Kendall and Chloe will be the understudies for Maddie, the only one of the group who, as of right now, is assured of dancing at Nationals.

The moms have had it up to HERE with Abby and Maddie’s star treatment, which you probably could’ve predicted if you watch Dance Moms online.

 

They decide to track the number of negative marks that the rest of the girls get against all of the positive feedback that Maddie receives. The results?

Maddie gets seven marks for praise while Kendall, Chloe and Nia all get complaints. Then Jill shows up with a white board to take the scoring to a new level.

Abby Lee Miller goes upstairs to give the moms a piece of her mind about this, and after hearing Jill out, sees it as the moms being jealous of Maddie.

Standard. She tells Jill that if she’s upset, Cathy’s right next door.

Cathy, meanwhile, is still upset over "getting robbed" the last time she competed against Abby and Co. and even calls her out over the loud speaker.

Gino and Maddie finally go toe-to-toe on the dance floor and when it comes to the latter’s performance, Abby is moved to tears by its beauty. Breathtaking.

The moms are STILL upset about the white board, which Abby chalks up to her teaching style and threatens to take Nia’s solo away next week.

Holly tells Abby that she’s ugly and walks out. Christi and Melissa follow, and Jill isn’t far behind. As they talk, Holly asks the camera crew to leave.

Abby, meanwhile, PULLS THE ROUTINE because of the moms’ behavior, handing Cathy a win she may or may not have earned against Miller.

That’s one way to make a point and show them who’s in control. As for the solos, Gino wins second place, while Maddie wins the overall first place.

Maddie is happy. She’s the only one. Abby tells the moms that they will not have another dressing room encounter after the Kelly Hyland fiasco.

Holly tells Abby her standards are higher than Abby’s, but Abby won’t listen to the criticism, beaching Nia’s solo as Holly tells her to get some class.

Good luck with that request.
